VALORANT

About the Game

Released in June 2020 by Riot Games, Valorant is a 5v5 character-based tactical shooter. It combines precise gunplay with diverse ability sets. Set in a near-future Earth, players control "Agents" who are members of the Valorant Protocol, defending the world against Radiant threats.

How to Play

The game is played in a round-based format between Attackers and Defenders.

  • The Spike: Attackers carry the Spike to plant it at a site. Defenders must stop them or defuse it within 45 seconds.
  • Economy: You earn credits every round to buy better weapons (like the Vandal or Phantom) and shields.
  • Abilities: Each Agent has a unique set of four abilities that can turn the tide of battle.

Match Structure & Scoring

Objective Requirement
Winning Condition First team to reach 13 rounds wins.
Side Swap Teams switch sides after round 12.
Elimination Winning a round by killing all 5 enemies.
Plant/Defuse Winning by successfully detonating or defusing the Spike.
