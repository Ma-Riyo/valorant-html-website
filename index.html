VALORANT

What is Valorant?

Valorant is a free-to-play first-person tactical hero shooter developed and published by Riot Games. It blends high-stakes gunplay with unique character abilities, requiring both precision aiming and strategic teamwork.

In the main game mode, players are assigned to either the Attacking or Defending team with the goal of planting or defusing the "Spike" in a best-of-24 round format.

Popular Agents

Agents are the playable characters in Valorant, each representing a specific role: Duelist, Controller, Initiator, or Sentinel.

Jett

A Duelist from South Korea with an agile fighting style that lets her take risks no one else can.

Sage

A Sentinel from China who creates safety for herself and her team wherever she goes.

Omen

A Controller who hunts in the shadows. He renders enemies blind and teleports across the field.
